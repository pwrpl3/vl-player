Premium UI/UX

Designed with modern aesthetics. Enjoy a fluid, intuitive interface that matches the quality of mainstream streaming platforms, entirely offline.

High-Speed Search

Instantly query massive local libraries. Optimized with Python to find your favorite tracks without lag, stutter, or loading screens.

FFmpeg Powered

Robust audio decoding seamlessly integrated via PySide6 to ensure flawless playback across your entire local directory.

High-Fidelity Audio

Experience uncompromised sound. Full support for lossless formats, high bitrates, and pristine playback quality for the true audiophile.

Discord Rich Presence

Show off your taste. Seamlessly broadcast your current playing track, artist, and album directly to your Discord profile in real-time.

Dynamic Theming

Built-in support for beautifully crafted Dark and Light modes that adapt perfectly to your desktop environment—whether you are on Windows or Linux.
